The German Dual Apprenticeship System

How it Works.

The German dual Apprenticeship system in Pittsburgh and can currently offer four different training programs. The goal is to train high school students as well as other employees who already work in the company, through the apprenticeship program and to provide them with the skills and abilities needed in the company. It is a very good alternative to avoid the high costs of studying as companies pay for the tuition costs during the training period.
