How Apprenticeships Work

In-house Skills Development

Employers enabled to drive skills development to meet immediate workforce needs and along industry-based competencies.

May include up to 6,000 hours of On-the-Job Training (OJT).
Related Technical Instruction

Rigorous course-formatted learning delivered by GACC's exclusive network of Community Colleges, industry-recognized course providers and skills development customized to meet employers' needs.

Industry-recognized Certifications

Apprentices who complete one of the four GACC programs earn the nationally recognized designation of journeyperson with a number of exclusive industry certificates and German credentials.
