Pre-Apprenticeships

GACC Pre-Apprenticeships support young at heart workers with part-time paid jobs that develop basic and better tech skills. Pre-apprentices are put on track to be hired as a full-time employee or may enter one of GACC's Registered Apprenticeships, like Sales Engineering.

Upside:
On-the-Job Training, Industry-recognized Certificates, Invaluable Workplace Experience, Pipeline into Apprenticeships & Employment, Employer Investment, Join the GACC Family.
