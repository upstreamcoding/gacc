What is an Apprenticeship?

Registered apprenticeship programs are structured, occupational training programs that combine...

...On-The-Job training with related technical instruction (i.e., coursework) with targeted goal to increases skill level and ability to perform skilled job effectively and safely. Apprenticeships follow clear wage scales that progressively increase over time or upon skill acquisition.

The Dual Apprenticeship program include the skills and competencies required from the German IHK for the job profiles and modified into a two-year program to fit the U.S. market.
