About Us

About Us

The German American Chamber of Commerce, Pittsburgh Chapter (GACC PIT) was founded in 2015 to promote and support bilateral trade between Germany and the U.S. We are a private, non-profit organization and serve as the official representative of German trade in Western Pennsylvania. We are part of an international network composed of 140 German foreign chambers of commerce and government offices in 92 countries. Members enjoy the benefits of networking events, a professional team with transatlantic expertise, visibility for their brand on our digital and print channels, exclusive invitations to events and conferences, publications and databases, visa and legal services, career support, and more.

Dual Apprenticeship system

In addition to the mentioned services, we are implementing the German Dual Apprenticeship system in Pittsburgh and can currently offer four different training programs. The goal is to train high school students as well as other employees who already work in the company, through the apprenticeship program and to provide them with the skills and abilities needed in the company. It is a very good alternative to avoid the high costs of studying as companies pay for the tuition costs during the training period.
