As part of our mission to drive economic development in the region and the ever-growing debate about a skill gap in Pittsburgh and beyond, we initiated our workforce programs to help "close the gap".

Apprenticeships

The Apprenticeship program gives high school graduates, but also employees already working in a company, the opportunity to benefit from the German-based dual vocational training concept, which merges classroom training at a local community with hands-on work experiences at a company.

View Apprenticeships

Pre-Apprenticeships

Pre-Apprenticeship is designed to prepare students to enter and succeed in registered Apprenticeship programs. The program gives High School students a paid internship opportunity to get hands-on experience at a company and receive an industry recognized certificate (e.g. MT1 from the MS Institute). Pre-apprentices can upon completion accelerate into one of our four Registered Apprenticeship programs or enter the workforce with technical skills that are in high demand.

Learn more

Train-the-Trainer Workshop

Based on the Gold Standard German "Ausbildung der Ausbilder" and German system of workforce training, our Train-the-Trainer workshop develops skilled workers into mentors and vision leaders.
